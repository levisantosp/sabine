Sabine is your all-in-one Valorant simulator and esports coverage Discord bot.

Stay updated with live matches, results, and news from both **VALORANT** and **League of Legends** esports. Manage your own team of player cards, compete in a detailed **PVP match simulator**, and participate in a global economy with **match predictions** and **betting**.
